Girl Boss Toxins, Inc. is a commercial networking concept that asks whether burgeoning entrepreneurs want to be their own boss. Those that say "yes" are welcomed into an MLM that keeps them stuck in a cycle of debt and taking advantage of others as they hawk their potions and poultices.
