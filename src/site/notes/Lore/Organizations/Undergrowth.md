Underground spy network working to undermine the monarchy. They're a decentralized organization, with no true leader and no members list. Members make connections to a few others by being inducted, but the membership overall is secret and unknown to any one member. 

Members receive a [[Journal/Tome of the Undegrowth\|Tome of the Undegrowth]] which provides them a method of communicating with one another magically.
