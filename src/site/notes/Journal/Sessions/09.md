# Chronicles

(x:: The party makes their way into [[Lore/Locations/Court of Crowns/The Citadel\|The Citadel]] and travel along the outer ring in search of the [[Lore/Locations/Court of Crowns/Prone Pixie\|Prone Pixie]]. They see a missing poster of a man named [[Characters/The Citadel/Frederick\|Frederick]] that looks the spitting image of the man they saved from the goblins and bugbears outside of the city. The visit the inn listed on the poster, the Sleeping Siren, to investigate.)

(x:: On the way, they get stopped by an artist who becomes enamored with [[Characters/Players/Duke\|Duke]]. She sketches an image of her new muse while they discuss the city, and she offers her life savings (3 copper) for taking his time to make the drawing. He refuses, and they end up agreeing to split the profits of the prints she makes of her nude drawing of him.)

(x:: [[Characters/Players/One-Eyed Hilda Thee Bitch\|Hilda]] runs into a branch of [[Lore/Organizations/Girl Boss Toxins, Inc.\|Girl Boss Toxins, Inc.]], the MLM she was tricked into joining by a previous patron of *her* inn. The party enters and talks with the proprietor of the shop, [[Characters/The Citadel/Dolga\|Dolga]]. Dolga tries to get Hilda to pay her subscription fee, but Hilda refuses. Dolga breaks down and explains that this business isn't what they think. That her family has been imprisoned, and the gnomes are required to make all the products as slaves. She begs the party to try to free her family. She doesn't know who is at the top of the pyramid, but she knows that there's a Girl Boss Toxins warehouse in the Middle Ring.)

(x:: The party questions [[Firga\|Firga]], the mother of the man [[Characters/The Citadel/Frederick\|Frederick]] who is on the missing posters. She says he's been gone a week, and has never not come home before. She's worried sick, and so is his fiancée, [[Firella\|Firella]].)
(x:: The party finally makes it to the [[Lore/Locations/Court of Crowns/Prone Pixie\|Prone Pixie]], and they enjoy an enchanting performance by [[Characters/Undergrowth/Miskinel Dewdrop\|Miski]], aka "Misty Step." In talking with her, they realize she is [[Characters/Undergrowth/Mordecai\|Mordecai]]'s contact in the [[Lore/Organizations/Undergrowth\|Undergrowth]], and she invites them to talk backstage after the show.)
(x:: When [[Characters/Undergrowth/Miskinel Dewdrop\|Miski]] heads backstage, they see the man they saved siting with his hood up! The man they believe to be [[Characters/The Citadel/Frederick\|Frederick]]. They confront him, and he denies it. He introduces himself of [[Characters/Court of Crowns/Melindir\|Melindir]], Prince of the [[Lore/Organizations/Court of Crowns\|Court of Crowns]] and son of Lady [[Characters/Court of Crowns/Orissa\|Orissa]]. He says he was looking for [[Characters/The Citadel/Raelor\|Raelor]], as he likes to come to the Pixie for the shows. He tells the party that, if they find Raelor, to let him know that Mel is looking for him.)

(x:: [[Characters/Undergrowth/Miskinel Dewdrop\|Miski]] takes them back stage and tells them what she knows. That [[Characters/Court of Crowns/Orissa\|Orissa]]'s army is preparing for something, that there have been a few men that match [[Characters/Court of Crowns/Melindir\|Melindir]]'s description that have gone missing recently, and that the Queen Regent, [[Characters/Court of Crowns/Sterella\|Sterella]], is [[Characters/Court of Crowns/Orissa\|Orissa]]'s. cousin and deep in her pocket. She thinks that this is a clear sign Orissa is staging a coup, but she doesn't know how the disappearances factor in. Miski says the party needs to get access to the inner rings to investigate, and the only person she knows that can get them inside is [[Characters/Silver Jackals/Felix Graves\|Felix Graves]].)
