 **The Road to the Citadel**
# Chronicles
(x:: The party, having made it out of the [[Lore/Locations/Glamazonia/Hall of Valor\|Hall of Valor]], finds themself on the Road to [[Lore/Locations/Court of Crowns/The Citadel\|The Citadel]]. They get a message from [[Journal/Tome of the Undergrowth/Mordecai - Stage Town\|Mordecai - Stage Town]] in the [[homebrew/personal/Tome of the Undergrowth\|Tome of the Undergrowth]] pointing them to his [[Lore/Organizations/Undergrowth\|Undergrowth]] contact in the city. He tells them to be on the lookout for a "*chartreuse chanteuse"* in the [[Lore/Locations/Court of Crowns/Prone Pixie\|Prone Pixie]].)

(x:: Along the pathway to the Citadel, the party is attacked by a band of Harpies. All but Garland succumb to the Harpies' song, but they ultimately dispatch them and take a well-deserved rest in the shade of a hill. The next day, they find themselves at the farm of a local fairy. [[Characters/The Citadel/Farmer Gann\|Farmer Gann]] is working in the field, and explains tot he party that the Citadel has increased the quota of wheat and he's having to work day and night to meet the requirements. Normally, he'd rely on his son, [[Characters/The Citadel/Raelor\|Raelor]], but he's been called up by the army. He doesn't know what or why, but he thinks something is going on and that war could be on the horizon.) 

(x:: The party agrees to help the old farmer harvest the crops, and he thanks them with a hearty meal and a place to sleep. [[Characters/Players/One-Eyed Hilda Thee Bitch\|Hilda]], unable to sleep, takes a night stroll in the fields and stumbles upon a small bird of prey with an injured wing. She brings the bird, dubbed [[Albert\|Albert]], back to the farmhouse. She gets [[Characters/Players/Garland Sagittarius\|Garland]] to help bandage the bird the next day, and [[Characters/The Citadel/Farmer Gann\|Farmer Gann]] lends the party a cart and some horses to get them to the city faster.) 

(x:: The party departs the farmer, and they come across a scene of several Bugbears and a goblin trying to tie up a young [[z_Data/CLI Data/compendium/races/eladrin-mpmm\|Eladrin]] man. The party intervenes as one of the Bugbears knocks the man unconscious. [[Characters/Players/Jet H. Blue\|Jet]] summons a mighty sleet storm that knocks the Bugbears prone and makes it difficult for them to move. The party picks them off one by one and successfully rescue the man.) 

(x:: He thanks you for saving him from those "Glassholes" trying to kidnap him. He's tall, muscular if not a bit slight. Sharp green eyes and proud shoulders. He has a crest on his lapel, a white tower in a red field beneath a blazing sun. The party notices he seems to be highborn, but fails to ask his name or who he is.) 

(x:: He explains he was going to visit a friend, the farmer's son [[Characters/The Citadel/Raelor\|Raelor]]. The party tells him that Raelor isn't home, that his father said he was called back into the city to serve in some sort of army mission or exercise. The man is confused and bids the party farewell before sprinting away and taking flight back towards The Citadel.)
