Son of [[Characters/Court of Crowns/Orissa\|Orissa]] of the Citadel, and heir to her throne. He's in love with [[Characters/The Citadel/Raelor\|Raelor]], a Farmer's son. They met in training for the Citadel's army, all young citizens are required to serve. Goes by "Mel" to his friends. 

#### Description
Tall, muscular figure if not a bit slight. Sharp green eyes and proud shoulders. Quick to smile but a bit rigid. Melts in the presence of [[Characters/The Citadel/Raelor\|Raelor]]. 

#### Power
He has an ancestral ability to wreathe his bodies in flame, making himself immune to fire and able to spout streams of fire from his hand. He can also fly.
